Our Story · No. II

Fragrance,
guided by
the stars.

RUDRA was born from a simple belief — that scent is a quiet kind of medicine. For centuries, ittar has been used in India not only as perfume, but as ritual: to calm the mind, lift the spirit, and align the wearer with something larger than themselves.

We took that tradition and asked a new question. What if a fragrance was made for you — not for the season, not for the trend, but for the energy of your sign?

A perfume for the chart you were born under.

Each RUDRA ittar is hand-blended in small batches. We start with pure botanical extracts — no alcohol, no synthetic fillers — and rest each blend on a sandalwood base for a fortnight before bottling. What you wear is what came out of the still: just the notes most attuned to your zodiac, distilled into a 12 ml glass bottle.

The result is a small library of twelve. One ittar for the fire signs, one for the water; one for the air, one for the earth. An atlas in scent — a way of marking the seasons of a life, and of a sky.

We make them slowly. We label them by hand. We ship them across India in a recycled paper box with a tag that says, simply, the name of your sign. Beyond that, the perfume does the talking.

Made slowly. Labelled by hand. Shipped in paper.

RUDRA is a project of RVK Ventures, a small house in India dedicated to honest, slow-made things. We believe in fewer products, made better; in shipping in paper, not plastic; and in the long quiet pleasure of a single drop, applied with intention.
